About Jelita Salon

Jelita Salon is one of the beauty salons that provides hair care services and so on, especially in the Cengkareng area, West Jakarta. Jelita Salon was established in February 2001. The increasing economy has an impact on increasing business growth in Indonesia. Business growth in recent years has been marked by a wide variety of competition in all fields, including the beauty salon business. The salon service business itself is a business that can be used as a long-term business, because the physical needs for a woman and even a man in recent years have begun to be used as a lifestyle by some people. Therefore, nowadays salons have become a basic need that must be met as a place for body care and beauty.
